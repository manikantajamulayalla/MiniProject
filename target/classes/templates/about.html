About FeedLink

Bridging the Gap Between Food Donors and Those in Need

Our Mission

FeedLink is an innovative web application designed to tackle food waste by connecting food donors with individuals and organizations in need. Our platform enables seamless redistribution of surplus food from restaurants, grocery stores, and households to local communities facing food insecurity.

We strive to reduce food waste, support communities, and promote transparency through real-time tracking and feedback systems.

Key Features

Donor-Friendly Interface

Allows individuals, restaurants, and businesses to easily list surplus food items for donation.

Recipient Access

Recipients can browse available food offerings based on location, type of food, and expiration date.

Real-Time Tracking

Ensures transparency and safety throughout the food donation process.

Scalability

Built to handle large volumes of donations, ensuring growth with demand.

Feedback Systems

Recipients can provide feedback, ensuring the quality and safety of donations.
